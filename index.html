Problem Statement: Implement and test self-service password reset (SSPR) capabilities to reduce helpdesk workload and empower users.

Approach: Enabled and configured Microsoft Entra Privileged Identity Management, assigned eligible roles, and tested role activation workflows with approvals.

Tools Used: Microsoft Entra PIM, Azure AD, Role Settings, Notifications

Key Lessons Learned: Understood how PIM minimizes the risk of privilege abuse and ensures accountability through access reviews and audit logs.
